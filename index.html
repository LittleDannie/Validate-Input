Personal Information

Full Name must be in letters only, be separated by a space, not less than 2 characters and not more than 40 characters 20 each

Password should contain letters, numbers, _@-$%& only and be between 8 to 12 characters

Telephone should start with 070,080,090,091 and 11 digits

NIN should be 11 digits only
